## Purpose

The Human-AI Collaboration Subcommittee advises on how humans and AI agents work together effectively. This includes intervention points, handoff design, trust calibration, and user experience.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Human intervention points
- Handoff design
- Trust calibration
- Cognitive load management
- Error communication
- Supervision patterns
- User experience of automation

### Out of Scope
- Agent architecture (→ Agent Architecture)
- Technical implementation
- Ethics (→ Ethics & Compliance)

---

## Key Questions We Address

1. When should the human intervene?
2. How do we communicate AI limitations to the user?
3. What handoffs between human and AI are needed?
4. How do we calibrate user trust in the system?
5. What cognitive load does the system place on the user?
