## Purpose

The Knowledge Management Subcommittee ensures that committee knowledge is properly organized, documented, and discoverable. They maintain the knowledge base and documentation standards.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Knowledge base organization
- Documentation standards
- Information architecture
- Discoverability
- Taxonomy and categorization

### Out of Scope
- Session recording (→ Secretary)
- Historical analysis (→ Historical Analysis)
- Technical documentation (content)

---

## Key Questions We Address

1. How should this knowledge be organized?
2. Can people find what they need?
3. What documentation standards should we follow?
4. Is important knowledge being captured?
5. How do we prevent knowledge loss?
