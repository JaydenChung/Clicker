## Purpose

The Recruitment Industry Subcommittee provides perspective from the recruiting and hiring side of the equation. They help the committee understand how recruiters think, what they're looking for, and how automation affects the hiring process.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Recruiter workflows and priorities
- Hiring pipeline mechanics
- Application volume impacts
- Candidate evaluation criteria
- Industry norms and expectations
- Red flags that trigger rejection
- Best practices for applications

### Out of Scope
- Technical automation details
- ATS system mechanics (technical)
- Resume formatting (â†’ Resume Optimization)

---

## Key Questions We Address

1. How do recruiters view automated applications?
2. What makes an application stand out (or get rejected)?
3. How does application volume affect success?
4. What timing is best for applications?
5. How do recruiters filter candidates?
6. What signals matter most to hiring teams?

## Key Insights This Committee Provides

1. **Volume Perspective**: Recruiters see hundreds of applications; standing out matters
2. **Timing**: When you apply can affect whether you're seen
3. **Quality Signals**: What makes recruiters pause vs. skip
4. **Automation Detection**: Recruiters may notice patterns
5. **Follow-up**: What happens after application submission
