## Key Committee Perspectives

### The Volume Reality
"Recruiters see 200+ applications for popular roles. You have seconds to make an impression."

### The Automation Concern
"If your application looks automated—identical to hundreds of others—it signals low interest."

### The Quality Signal
"Customization signals genuine interest. Even small touches matter."

### The Timing Factor
"Early applications often get more attention before recruiters are overwhelmed."
