## Creating a New Subcommittee

If a new standing or ad-hoc subcommittee is needed:

1. Chair proposes formation with charter
2. Committee discusses scope and membership
3. Human approves
4. Secretary creates folder structure
5. Members are assigned
6. Subcommittee begins work
