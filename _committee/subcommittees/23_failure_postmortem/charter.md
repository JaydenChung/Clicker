## Purpose

The Failure Postmortem Subcommittee analyzes failures after they occur to understand root causes and prevent recurrence. They practice blameless postmortems and focus on systemic improvement.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Root cause analysis
- Incident review
- Pattern identification in failures
- Prevention strategies
- Blameless postmortem facilitation
- Systemic issue identification

### Out of Scope
- Predicting failures (→ Red Team)
- Reliability design (→ Reliability Engineering)
- Active incident response

---

## Key Questions We Answer

1. What actually happened?
2. What was the root cause?
3. How do we prevent this from happening again?
4. Are there systemic issues contributing to failures?
5. What can we learn from this?

---

## Operating Mode

This subcommittee is called after failures to conduct blameless postmortems:
1. Gather facts without blame
2. Construct timeline
3. Identify contributing factors
4. Find root cause(s)
5. Recommend preventive measures
6. Document for future reference
