## Purpose

The Historical Analysis Subcommittee ensures that past sessions, decisions, and lessons learned inform current deliberations. They prevent the committee from repeating past mistakes and ensure institutional memory.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Past session analysis
- Decision history review
- Pattern identification
- Lesson extraction
- Precedent research
- Trend analysis

### Out of Scope
- Current session recording (→ Secretary)
- Knowledge base maintenance (→ Knowledge Management)
- Technical implementation

---

## Key Questions We Answer

1. Have we discussed this before? What was decided?
2. What patterns are emerging across sessions?
3. What lessons from past failures apply here?
4. What precedents should guide this decision?
5. How have similar approaches worked in the past?

---

## Operating Mode

This subcommittee is often called to provide historical context:
- Before major decisions
- When proposals seem familiar
- After failures (what can we learn?)
- During strategic planning
