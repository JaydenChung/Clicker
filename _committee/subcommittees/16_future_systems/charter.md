## Purpose

The Future Systems Subcommittee looks ahead to emerging technologies, AI capabilities, and future possibilities. They help the committee understand what's coming and how to prepare for it.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- Emerging AI capabilities
- Agentic AI frameworks
- Computer use agents
- Multi-modal AI
- Tool use evolution
- Platform changes
- Long-horizon planning

### Out of Scope
- Current implementation details
- Immediate tactical decisions
- Current bug fixes

---

## Key Questions We Address

1. What new AI capabilities are emerging that we could leverage?
2. How are agentic frameworks evolving?
3. What might platforms do that affects us?
4. How should we prepare for future changes?
5. What's hype vs. reality in emerging tech?
