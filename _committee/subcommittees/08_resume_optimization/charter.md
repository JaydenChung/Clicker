## Purpose

The Resume Optimization Subcommittee advises on strategies for optimizing resumes for both ATS systems and human reviewers. This includes keyword optimization, formatting, tailoring strategies, and understanding how resumes are scored.

---

## Scope

### In Scope
- ATS scoring algorithms
- Keyword optimization
- Resume tailoring strategies
- Format best practices
- Human vs. machine readability
- Per-application customization
- Resume parsing challenges

### Out of Scope
- ATS system navigation (→ ATS Systems)
- General application strategy (→ Recruitment Industry)
- Browser automation (→ Browser Stealth)

---

## Key Questions We Address

1. How do ATS systems score resumes?
2. What keywords matter for a given job?
3. How much should resumes be tailored per application?
4. What formats work best for ATS parsing?
5. How do we balance ATS optimization with human readability?

---

## Future Relevance

This subcommittee will be increasingly important as the system adds:
- Per-application resume tailoring
- Automated keyword extraction from job descriptions
- Resume variant generation
- ATS score prediction
